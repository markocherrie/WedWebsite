Car/Taxi
Please direct yourself/the driver to Hawes Pier (South Queensferry, EH30 9TB). There is free parking at Hawes Pier and the Scout Hall if you want to drive between the two. Some local Taxi services are;
  • Dalmeny Cars (0131 300 0767)
  • Jimmy's Private Hire (07866570212)
Bus
If you are coming from Edinburgh then the 43 Lothian bus (£1.70 fare) goes from St. Andrews Square. It takes approximately 50 minutes in total, which includes a short walk from Walker Drive.
Train
ScotRail run trains from Edinburgh Waverly to Dalmeny, which is an 8 minute walk /2 minute taxi to Hawes Pier.
Ferry
Once you have arrived in Hawes Pier then you will be embarking on the Cherrie Ferry, a once in a lifetime charter down the Forth. Bring a brolly as our Scottish weather could go either way…!
